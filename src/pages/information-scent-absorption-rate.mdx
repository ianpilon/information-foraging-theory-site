# Information Scent Absorption Rate
**Summary**: Information Scent Absorption Rate (ISAR) is a method that measures the navigability of a site by computing the probability of users reaching the desired destinations on the site.
**Sources**: The Bloodhound Project- Automating Discovery of Web Usability Issues using the InfoScent™ Simulator.pdf
**Last updated**: 2026-05-27
---
## Introduction
ISAR is a new method introduced by the Bloodhound Project to measure the navigability of a site.
## Calculation
ISAR is calculated by computing the probability of users reaching the desired destinations on the site.
